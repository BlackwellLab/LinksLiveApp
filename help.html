Starting and Joining Rounds

From the New tab, choose Start Round. Pick a course (search by name or location), choose a scoring mode, and — for 18-hole courses — choose whether you're playing the Front 9, Back 9, or all 18. Tap Create Round when you're ready.

To join someone else's round, ask them for their 6-character join code and enter it on the Join tab. Use the Round/Tournament toggle at the top if you're joining a tournament instead of a single round.

Scoring a Round

On the scoring screen, use the +/− buttons to enter strokes and putts for each hole. LinksLive automatically shows a badge for the hole (Eagle, Birdie, Par, Bogey, Double+) and a GIR (Green in Regulation) tag when you hit the green in the right number of strokes. Your running total vs. par updates as you go, and the hole dots at the top show your score history for the round at a glance.

Everything saves automatically as you move between holes — there's no separate save button. Tap Finish Round on the last hole to complete it and see your summary.

Scoring Modes & Handicap

When starting a round, you choose one of three modes:

  • Stroke Play — straightforward gross scoring.
  • Handicap — enter your handicap index once (LinksLive remembers it for next time) and the app calculates your net score using the World Handicap System, allocating strokes to holes by difficulty when the course has that data. You'll see an "Est." label on net scores since a self-reported index is an estimate, not an official one.
  • Match Play — 1-on-1 only. A live status chip shows things like "2 UP thru 7" or "All Square" as the round progresses.

Courses & Favorites

Don't see your course in the list? Tap the + button next to "Select Course" to add it — enter the name, location, and par/yardage per hole. Any LinksLive user can add or fix course data, so if something looks wrong (wrong par, missing yardage), tap "Edit" on that course to correct it for everyone.

Play somewhere often? Swipe right on a course in the list to star it as a favorite. Favorited courses float to the top of the list so you don't have to search for them every time.

Inviting Others

When creating a round or tournament, enter a friend's email address to invite them. They need to already have a LinksLive account with that email verified — if they don't, ask them to download the app, sign up, and verify their email first. Once they've done that, you'll be able to find and add them.

You can invite up to 5 players per round or tournament this way.

Tournaments

From the New tab, choose Start Tournament to set up a multi-round event. Pick a format — Stroke Play or Stableford, each available gross or net (with handicap) — and invite players the same way as a round.

The tournament hub shows live standings as rounds are played, plus the list of rounds within the tournament. As the commissioner (the person who created it), you can add new rounds to the tournament or close it when it's finished.

Managing Rounds, Data & Your Account

Removing a round or tournament: swipe left on a card on the Home screen and tap the trash icon. If it's just you (a solo round), this deletes it entirely. If others are involved, this removes you from it without affecting other players. Commissioners of a multi-player tournament can't remove themselves this way — close the tournament instead.

Clear My Data (Settings → Danger): removes all of your solo rounds/tournaments and your participation in shared ones, without deleting your account.

Delete Account (Settings → Danger): permanently deletes your account and all associated data. This can't be undone.
