Data Deletion

LinksLive gives you two ways to delete your data, both from within the app under Settings.

Delete specific data ("Clear My Data")

Go to Settings → Clear My Data. This permanently deletes rounds and tournaments you created solo. For rounds or tournaments shared with other players, it removes you from the player list and deletes your personal scores, without deleting the round or tournament for other participants. Your account (email, name) is kept.

Delete your entire account

Go to Settings → Delete Account. You'll be asked to confirm your identity (password, or Google sign-in). This permanently deletes your account, your profile data (email, name, handicap), and all score data tied to your account. This action cannot be undone.

Both deletions take effect immediately — there is no additional retention period.

Frequently Asked Questions

I didn't get my verification email. What do I do?

Check your spam/junk folder first — verification and password-reset emails sometimes land there. If you still don't see it, open the app and use the "Resend" option on the verification screen, or email us and we can help directly.

How do I join a round or tournament?

Ask whoever started the round or tournament for their 6-character join code, then use the Join tab in the app and enter the code.

How do I invite a friend?

When creating a round or tournament, enter your friend's email address. They need to already have a LinksLive account with that email verified — if they don't, download LinksLive, sign up, and verify the email first, then you'll be able to add them.

How do I reset my password?

On the login screen, tap "Forgot Password?" and enter your email. Check your spam folder if the reset email doesn't arrive within a few minutes.

How do I delete my account or clear my data?

See Data Deletion above for full details.

Is my scoring data private?

Your scores are only visible to players you've shared a round or tournament with. See our Privacy Policy for full details on what we collect and how it's used.

For anything else, reach us at support@blackwelllab.com.
